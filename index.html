Welcome to our website

Are you an alumni at Bernice university interested in campus events, professional development and connecting with fellow alumni? You have come to the right place. Our website aims to foster a strong network among alumni, support career advancement and build an engaging, strong community.

We provide an events calendar featuring alumni gatherings, workshops, webinars and more where you can RSVP directly on our website. We also send timely event reminders and updates via email so you never miss out!

We also post job openings and internship opportunities specifically targeted towards alumni, resume building tips, interview preparation resources and career counseling services. Our website also facilitates mentorship programs where experienced alumni can mentor recent graduates or others seeking career guidance.
